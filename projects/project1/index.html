About Us

Blue Orb college has been in existence for over ten years. Our ideals are rooted in the need to provide quality and affordable education for our future leaders. We ensure that your wards do not just get the best in class academic guidance but also understand the need to be a well balanced individual in the society. Morals, ethics, teamwork and social equity are as important to an individual's as his intelligence and academic performances. These qualities form the bedrock of the Blue Orbs academic curriculum. Our student are also encouraged to join and fully participate in our Jets or Press clubs activities while also devoting ample time for sporting activities as well. At Blue Orb college we believe that only an healthy mind can study effectively. Would'nt you rather Join us today?
